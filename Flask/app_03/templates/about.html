About This Superstore Recommender App

📊 This app helps businesses suggest relevant products to customers based on their buying history and similar customer behavior.

🔍 It analyzes the Orders sheet to find common purchasing patterns and gives smart product suggestions.

✅ Supports entering up to 3 Customer IDs at once, so you can compare recommendations for multiple customers easily!

🚀 Great for personalized marketing, upselling, and understanding customer segments.

📈 Future features may include customer clustering, dashboards, and real-time recommendations.
