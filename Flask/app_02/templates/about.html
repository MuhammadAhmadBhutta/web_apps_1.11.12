📊 About This Tool

Welcome to the Customer Segmentation Tool — a simple, powerful web app that helps you understand your customers better.

This tool uses machine learning (K-Means clustering) to group your customers into meaningful segments based on their buying patterns, profit contribution, order frequency, and more.

Businesses use segmentation to:

  • 🏷️ Run targeted marketing campaigns
  • 🔍 Identify loyal VIPs & high spenders
  • 📈 Boost customer retention
  • 💡 Make smarter, data-driven decisions

Simply upload your customer dataset and get instant insights — so you can know your customers and grow your business.
